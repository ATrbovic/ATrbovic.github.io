Works in Progress

Academic research and ongoing work

  • Dynamic Quantum Markov Games: A Framework for Adversarial Multi-Agent Coordination with Fluid Team Boundaries
    Alex Trbovic · In Preparation · Florida State University · Advisor: Lichun Li
    Introduces the DQMG multi-layer architecture and establishes formal existence and uniqueness conditions for equilibria in adversarial multi-agent systems with dynamic team composition.
  • Triggered Reorganization in Multi-Agent Systems: An Order Parameter Approach to Structural Adaptation
    Alex Trbovic · In Preparation · Florida State University · Advisor: Lichun Li
    Formalizes a reorganization mechanism governed by an entropy-based order parameter κ, characterizing the phase transition between coherent and degraded team states in adversarial environments.
  • Quantum Coordination Mechanisms in Adversarial Games: Beyond Classical Communication Constraints
    Alex Trbovic · In Preparation · Florida State University · Advisor: Lichun Li
    Examines whether quantum information protocols expand the set of achievable coordination outcomes for teams operating under limited communication, with applications to multi-agent reinforcement learning.
  • Bridging Team Cognition and Game Theory: A Formal Model of Collective Decision-Making Under Disruption
    Alex Trbovic · In Preparation · Florida State University · Advisor: Lichun Li
    Develops a game-theoretic formalization of team cognition dynamics, grounding empirical measures of team coordination in the DQMG framework and connecting psychological order parameters to equilibrium behavior.
